Why do we do what we do

The Key Problems we Solve for our Customers

Our job is to solve these core challenges sustainably and help our customers drive proactive growth in their target market segments.

01 We don‘t grow in spite of everything we‘ve already done & tried.

What matters most is the order in which you tackle the challenges. With our experience from more than 50 projects, you learn this sequence and uncover the root cause of why growth has stalled so far. You get a facilitator for your growth process who guides you to your target state.

02 Our sales is too reactive.

Proactivity isn’t just a mindset - it’s a structured approach. You focus your resources on the right customers and opportunities and steer your sales teams with clear KPIs. The result: more time spent actively selling, less time wasted, fewer resources drained, and a sales engine that pulls the whole organization forward.

03 Our CRM system is barely used. We can’t rely on the data.

CRM data is only valuable when it reflects reality. You combine robust sales methodology with hands-on CRM implementation to help your team build a funnel they can trust. The reward: accurate forecasts, actionable insights, and better strategic decisions across the business.

04 I can‘t rely on the forecast numbers to sync with cash & my fulfillment.

Spotting market ups and downs early and being able to plan with sales data will be a real competitive advantage in 2026.
By staying close to customers and the market, and by working with your CRM intensively, you ensure your sales numbers become increasingly reliable.

05 We depend on a handful of key accounts. When they catch a cold, we get a fever.

Dependence on a few major customers is a given for many companies. But this dependence is becoming more and more of a risk. By proactively developing additional key accounts, you spread the exposure across more shoulders and reduce the impact of any single customer.
